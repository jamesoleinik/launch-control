**Launch Control · Episode 12: Full Orchestra + Your Turn.**

Twelve episodes. One project. Six surfaces, all reading and writing the same Dataverse tables.

In the demo I run them back-to-back in 60 seconds:

1️⃣ **MCP** — natural-language query against the model
2️⃣ **Python SDK** — bulk update through pandas
3️⃣ **CLI** — solution operation from the terminal
4️⃣ **Declarative agent** (Copilot Studio) — conversational coordinator
5️⃣ **Autonomous agent** (Sentinel) — event-triggered escalation
6️⃣ **Code-first agent** (Python) — same skills, different runtime

One business process. One source of truth in Dataverse. Six ways to act on it.

That was the thesis the whole series: **mastering business process state in Dataverse — like a transactional dynamic-programming substrate — lets every agent, app, and Copilot stay coherent.**
