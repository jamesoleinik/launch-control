**Launch Control · Episode 10: Copilot Just Knows.**

Three episodes ago I shipped an agent. Today I'm taking it away.

Microsoft 365 Copilot can now answer questions directly from Dataverse — no agent, no Copilot Studio configuration, no plugin. Toggle **Dataverse Intelligence** on for the environment and the tables, and Copilot grounds its answers on your real launch data.

I asked it three questions in the demo:
• "What's the status of Smart Widget Pro?"
• "Which milestones are at risk this week?"
• "Who owns the open blockers?"

All three answered from live Dataverse rows. No agent in the middle.

The takeaway isn't "agents are dead." It's: **for read-mostly Q&A, the most powerful agent is the one you don't have to build.** Save the custom agents for the workflows where you actually need orchestration.
