**Launch Control · Episode 2: Your Playbook & Ingestion.**

Every team has a launch playbook. Most of them live in someone's head, a Notion page, or a 47-tab spreadsheet.

This episode, the playbook moves into Dataverse as **Business Skills** — portable rules an agent can follow at runtime.

Then the trickier half: ingesting the messy real-world data that should hydrate those rules. I didn't write a one-off importer. I built a **mapping-driven CLI ingestion path** so when the source schema changes (and it always does), you change the mapping, not the code.

The two things together — Business Skills + mapping-driven ingestion — are why the agents in later episodes stay sane when the data evolves.
